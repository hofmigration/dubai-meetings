The process

How it works

  1. 01

    Choose a slot

    Pick your name, the manager, a date and a time. Taken slots for that manager are greyed out.

  2. 02

    Manager approves

    The manager gets an email to approve the visit or mark themselves busy for that time.

  3. 03

    You're booked

    You get a confirmation the moment they approve. If they're busy, the slot frees up again.
